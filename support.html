Need help? We've got you.

How to register for trainings, find resources, make a donation, and reach the right team at REI.

Last updated June 19, 2026

Whether you want to bring a workshop to your organization, find a resource, or make a gift, here is how to get what you need from the Racial Equity Institute.

Get started

How to get help

  • Register for a training. Browse our trainings and conferences, then book through our scheduling system. Read the Guide to Hosting an REI Workshop before booking.
  • Find resources. Visit our resources page for the Groundwater Approach report and other materials.
  • Make a donation. Support the work through our donate page.
  • Reach the right person. Use our contact page to connect with the team for programs, scheduling, or accounting.
Response times

What to expect

When you submit a booking request, you will receive an instant confirmation of receipt by email. An email accepting or declining your request is typically sent within ten business days. For general questions sent to our team or contact form, we respond in a timely manner. Invoices are typically sent within two weeks of service, with payment due within 30 days.

Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Many common questions — about scheduling, trainers, travel, invoicing, cancellation, certificates, and materials — are answered on our FAQs page. It is the fastest way to find what you need before reaching out.

Still need a hand?

Make a request

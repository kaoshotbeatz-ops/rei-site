REI Debrief

Review and connect the concepts of a workshop to your organization’s work.

DurationHalf-day (3 hrs) or full-day (5.5 hrs)
CapacityCapped at a firm 35
FormatIn-person & virtual (Zoom)

REI offers debriefing sessions with groups who have experienced a Groundwater, Phase I, or Phase I and II workshop. Debriefs are based on the particular needs of groups or organizations. Participants review and examine the concepts presented, paying attention to how they relate to each other and to your organization’s work, all in relationship to REI’s analysis of structural racism. Discussions explore questions such as: How does the analysis explain our present circumstances? How has racism remade itself in contemporary times? Why is an organizing approach necessary to challenge or end racism? How do you apply workshop concepts to the work you do?
